Stress-free. Satisfaction guarantee.

Never worry about software updates or hardware issues with
RO1. Our team will support you every step of the way and
guarantee your RO1 is always in top working order.

On-site installation and setup. We’ll come on-site to understand your work environment and set up RO1 to perform the tasks you need it to.

Continuous software updates. We’re always working to make RO1 software, and you’ll get every update as soon as it's ready.

Remote access diagnostics. Need help troubleshooting your RO1? Our team will access it remotely over the internet to diagnose any issues.

Joint and hardware replacements. If you experience any hardware issues such as joints wearing down, we’ll replace the part at no additional cost.

Discounts on 3rd-party accessories. RO1 is compatible with a wide array of accessories, and you’ll get the best prices on them from us.

No commitment. If you change your mind about working with RO1, you can cancel your subscription at any time without penalty.
